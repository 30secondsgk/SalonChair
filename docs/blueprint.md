# **App Name**: SalonStack

## Core Features:

- Public Salon Directory & Appointment Booking: Users can browse and filter salons by location (State, City), view detailed Salon Cards including name, landmark, and service menu, and submit appointment requests for preferred slots.
- Salon Owner Onboarding & Profile Management: Salon owners can register their business, set up their salon profile, define a service menu with pricing, input landmark details, and manage shop information.
- Owner Booking Acceptance/Rejection: Salon owners receive notifications for new booking requests and can either accept or reject them through a dedicated interface, adhering to a 4-hour response timer.
- Subscription Payment & Status Management: Integrate a payment gateway for salon owners to pay the ₹200/month subscription fee, and automatically manage the 'Active'/'Hidden' status of their listings based on payment status.
- Admin Dashboard for Authorization & Monitoring: An admin interface to authorize new salon registrations, verify addresses, track overall revenue, and provide manual overrides to activate/deactivate salon visibility.
- Automated User Notifications with AI Recommendations: Send automated SMS/WhatsApp notifications to users regarding their booking status (accepted/rejected), and include an AI tool to suggest alternative nearby salons if their request is rejected.
- No-Show Flagging & User Ban System: Salon owners can flag users who fail to show up for appointments. Accumulation of three no-show flags results in a 30-day ban for the user.

## Style Guidelines:

- The application will utilize a light color scheme to convey a sense of professionalism and cleanliness, ideal for a service marketplace. The primary accent color is a muted indigo (#604BAC), chosen for its reliable and sophisticated character. This color is balanced by a very light, almost white, background (#F4F3F6), which carries a subtle hint of the primary hue for harmony. An analogous, brighter blue (#4F89F0) serves as an accent to highlight interactive elements and call-to-actions, providing clear contrast and visual interest.
- For headlines and prominent text, 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) will be used to impart a modern and slightly technical feel. Body text, and other descriptive content will utilize 'Inter' (sans-serif), ensuring excellent readability and a neutral, objective presentation across various screen sizes.
- The iconography will feature clean, modern line-art icons that are easily recognizable and align with the app's professional and streamlined aesthetic. Icons will support intuitive navigation and clearly represent service categories and booking actions.
- The layout will be designed for mobile-first responsiveness, featuring clean and organized salon cards for easy browsing. User and owner interfaces will use clear forms and structured data presentation to ensure intuitive interaction and efficient task completion.
- Subtle and purposeful animations will enhance the user experience, including smooth transitions between pages and sections, gentle hover effects on interactive elements, and discreet feedback animations for actions like form submissions and booking confirmations. This approach will contribute to a polished and responsive feel without distracting the user.
